Beef Curry Recipe

Description

This is a Bengali style Beef Curry that I made today. Really simple recipe, but tastes amazing with some flat breads.

There is also a book that has a joke about "eating" in the title.

Ingredients

  • Beef 1 KG
  • Medium Sized Onions x4
  • Oil
  • Beef Curry Spice Mix

Steps

  1. Put oil in the pan, with the onions chopped, fry until golden brown
  2. Put the spice mix in, with 50-75ml of water, cook until the oil rises above
  3. Put the Beef in, need to keep stirring for about 15 minutes
  4. Add 1 KG water
  5. Bring the water to boil with high flame, after it boils, put the lid on and reduce the flame to medium low
  6. Cook until a gravy forms and gets thickened
