How to make a perfect Omelette

Description

This is a simple Omelette

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Onions
  • Butter
  • Green Chilies & Pinch of Salt

Steps

  1. Finely chop Green Chilies and Onions
  2. Break and add 2 eggs along with the chopped stuff in a bowl
  3. Hit up the pan and but butter in it
  4. Put the mixture in, fry for 2 minutes, flip it, fry for 3 minutes and it is done
