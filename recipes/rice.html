How to make Steamed Rice

Description

This is a Bengali style Steamed Rice simple, yet fueling 99% people of Bangladesh everyday.

Ingredients

  • Rice 500 G
  • A big pot
  • Water
  • Pinch of Salt

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice until clean
  2. Add clean water until there is a 1 inch gap between rice and water
  3. Put on high flame until it comes to boil
  4. After that put it on medium flame for 20 minutes and it is ready!
